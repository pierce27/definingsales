About Us

Our History

Prior to Defining Sales, we all had individual careers as sales professionals across many different industries, selling very different products and services. Always wanting to help each other succeed, we would share individual success stories as to how sales results were increased through the utilization of technology, social media, lead generation, and marketing programs. Sharing our experiences allowed us to all achieve more success within our current roles, while providing an opportunity to package the knowledge we had acquired, and offer it to organizations seeking new, creative approaches to selling.

Realizing that there are so many technologies on the market to choose from, we made a conscious decision to identify the most useful tools that would bring success to business development teams, and share our knowledge to organizations looking to gain a competitive edge.

We planned to start slow as a side job, soliciting prospective customers that seeked customized solutions which would best address their business development needs. However we soon found that there was a huge market for the services being offered. So after hours of brainstorming, a few cocktails, and enough research to write 10 books, Defining Sales was born.
