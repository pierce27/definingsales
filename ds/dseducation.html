DS Education

Imagine being able to utilize technology flawlessly; imagine knowing shortcuts, best practices, and making the most out of your time spent in front of your devices. Not only would this prevent numerous headaches and stop unnecessary stress, it will allow a salesperson to make the most out of the day. Saving time and hours by understanding how to use technology properly, means more time spent building relationships with your prospects.

DS wants to help make time spent in front of a computer as productive as possible. Technology can be challenging, however with the proper knowledge of systems, and knowing how to best use them, your team will be able to spend less time frustrated and more time generating leads. Regardless of what software or systems your company utilizes, we will make sure you are using them to their full potential, resulting in time and money saved.

Computer Skills

We all spend more than half our workday looking at a screen. Whether it be your PC, Mac, tablet, or any other device, understanding how to unlock the full potential of the system is key to success and time management. DS recognizes this, and will help your organization’s sales team take advantage of your system’s complete capabilities. This will enable you to use technology as a tool to support your day to day sales efforts.

Sales Software

Adapting and truly understanding technology, old or new, can be beyond frustrating. With a broad spectrum of products on the market ranging from Social Media to CRM, it is important to understand how these technologies can be used to improve your sales efforts. Once understood, they’ll be a new found clarity on how proper software utilization can bring benefits to your sales team. DS has made it a priority to understand how to use these technologies correctly, and will share this knowledge with your team.

Self Sufficient Learning

Have you had problems understanding the instruction manuals that came with something you’ve purchased? Do you attempt to troubleshoot issues through trial and error just to avoid having to call an outsourced customer support center? DS knows how you feel, and we want to help you help yourself.

There are many resources on the web that can assist in troubleshooting software, learning to use an application, or understanding a process. Our experience from working with people of all ages and computer skill levels has brought awareness to the fact that many people underestimate themselves. Finding answers to your problems can be as easy as typing a few words, using a company FAQ, or networking online through a forum.

We will help your team learn how to take advantage of all the tools at hand to effectively solve problems, minimize troubleshooting time, and simplify your day. After all, the less time you spend solving problems, the more time you have to sell!
