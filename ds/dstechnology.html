DS Technology

Technology and sales go hand in hand. The ability to organize, maintain, and reach out to new prospects is key to growing a successful company. At DS we know how important this is, and at the same time how hard and time consuming it can be to utilize the best tools for your business development team.

Whether it be CRM’s, Email Content Marketing, Email Tracking, Analytics, File Sharing, or Telecom technologies, DS has the knowledge to make sure your team is implementing and using the best possible technology with the best possible ROI.

With the right tools in place, pipeline results can be maximized, knowledge can be gained, and new leads can be generated. Defining Sales can help determine which tools and technologies are right for your business development team to get the job done.

CRM

A lot of companies have CRM’s, but not a lot of teams use them the right way. With so many options in the market, using the right CRM for your organization can help your team track, record, and maintain your customer relationships. DS will make sure that your company is using the best technology for your needs, with the best ROI.

Lead Generation

Wish you had more leads? DS can help you get them. Through our knowledge of lead generation tools, you can fill up your database with quality contacts, allowing you to effectively reach a larger audience.

Email Marketing

There’s 100 ways to send emails, but only a few ways to make them count. With the correct email marketing software, not only can you reach the inbox of your contacts, you can create content based HTML emails quickly and easily, and know who is opening your messages.

Social Media

Social media is a great way to advertise, and create brand awareness. With so many platforms out there, it is often difficult to maintain all your accounts. DS will help you aggregate your social platforms and reach a broad audience.
